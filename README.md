# Pocket POS Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** April 14, 2026

Pocket POS is an offline-first point-of-sale application for small retail businesses. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app processes, how it is used, and how users can request account deletion.

## Information We Process

Pocket POS may process the following information:

- Phone number used for OTP-based authentication
- Store information entered by the user, such as store name, address, receipt footer, and tax settings
- Operational business records created in the app, including products, held carts, sales, refunds, receipt archives, and app preferences
- Authentication session information when online authentication services are configured

## How We Use Information

Pocket POS uses information to:

- Authenticate account access using OTP
- Protect local access using a PIN
- Save store settings and receipt configuration
- Manage products, inventory-related sales activity, held carts, refunds, receipts, and reports
- Support reliability, security, and account-management features

## Storage and Sharing

Pocket POS is designed around local business operation.

- Business records such as products, sales, refunds, receipts, held carts, and store settings are primarily stored locally on the user’s device
- Authentication-related data may be processed by configured backend services such as Supabase
- Information is not sold to third parties
- Information may be disclosed if required by law or necessary to protect the app, users, or service integrity

## Permissions

Pocket POS may request or use the following capabilities:

- **Camera**: used for barcode scanning
- **Internet access**: used for authentication and connected services

Pocket POS does **not** require permission to read SMS messages for normal OTP entry.

## Security

Pocket POS includes practical safeguards such as authentication, local PIN protection, and controlled account actions. However, no method of software, storage, or transmission is completely secure.

## Data Retention

Business records remain available in the app until they are deleted by the user or local app data is cleared. Authentication-related records are retained according to the configured backend service policies, where applicable.

## Account Deletion

If account deletion is available in the app, users can request deletion through the in-app **Delete Account** option in Settings.

Account deletion removes the authenticated account and clears relevant local business data from the device as part of the deletion flow.

## Children’s Privacy

Pocket POS is not directed to children and is intended for business use.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The latest public version should always be treated as the current version.
